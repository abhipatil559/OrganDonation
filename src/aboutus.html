■ Who we are?
We provide a organ donation service to people, looking after organ donation services in India. This includes managing the donation, storage of organs and researching new treatments and processes.
Our mission is to save and enhance the lives of others.With the support of our amazing donors, we give a lifeline to thousands of people every year. But there is always more to do. Which is why we continually strive to improve the safety, availability, quality and efficiency of our services.

■ What we do ?
‘What We Have Is Material, What We Do Stays Forever’it has been our constant endeavour to contribute to the best abilities to this noble initiative. We are committed to spreading awareness for the importance of cadaver organ donation. We were established for two purposes:

To spread awareness and make cadaver donation possible, by increasing the number of cadaver organ donations in India and facilitating the entire process. To prevent the alarming number of kidney and liver failure cases that happen in India and also provide them with a new lease of life.
